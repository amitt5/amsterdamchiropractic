Complaints
Health4Life Chiropractic treats a wide range of musculoskeletal complaints. Select a complaint for more information about causes, symptoms, and treatment.

Whiplash
Sudden neck movement due to accident or fall — neck pain, headache, dizziness, tingling.

Low back pain
80% of the population will experience lower back pain at some point. This can be a problem in muscles, joints, or discs.

Upper Back & Shoulder
Pain between the shoulder blades, radiating to the arms or chest. Often related to posture.

Neck
Stiff neck and shoulders, dizziness, tingling in hands. Gradual or sudden.

Hernia
Herniated disc (HNP) — bulging intervertebral disc, radiating pain in the leg or arm.

Pregnancy
Safe chiropractic care during pregnancy. Lower back pain, pelvic instability, nausea.

Sports injuries
Prevention and treatment of sports injuries. Athletes require a special approach.

Headache & Migraine
Migraines affect 1 in 10 adults. Chiropractic care treats the cause, not just the symptom.

Tension headache
A pressing pain like a band around the head, often originating in the neck. Stress and posture can be the cause.

Children
Children experience pain from falls, sports, or prolonged sitting. Recovery is faster than in adults.

Babies
KISS syndrome, excessive crying, sleep problems, feeding problems. Playful, child-friendly treatment.

Osteoarthritis / Wear and tear
Joint wear and tear due to aging. Chiropractic care can slow the deterioration and reduce pain.
