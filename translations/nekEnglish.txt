Neck pain
Common conditions include stiff neck and shoulder muscles, whiplash, muscle knots, osteoarthritis, and a herniated disc. Chiropractic care can provide relief for many neck complaints.

Symptoms
The most common neck complaints are nagging pain and stiffness. Other common complaints include:

Dizziness
Tinnitus
Concentration disorders
Radiating pain to arms
Weak or tired feeling in shoulders or arms
Tingling in hands or fingers
Causes
Neck pain can have several causes. The most common are:

Gradual causes
·
Little variation in posture (as with office work).
·
Little variety in movement (with monotonous work).
·
Incorrect or one-sided loading (such as a heavy bag over one shoulder).
·
The natural aging of the cartilage in the neck (also called osteoarthritis, wear and tear or degeneration).
·
Tension and stress.
·
Cold and draught.
Sudden causes
·
A fall or accident, which can result in whiplash.
·
Muscle cramps due to incorrect movement or twisting, incorrect sleeping position, or infection of the ear canal or airway.
